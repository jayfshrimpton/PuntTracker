# PuntTracker Beta User Guide

Welcome to the PuntTracker Beta! Thank you for helping us test the app. This guide will help you get started and let you know what to look out for.

## Getting Started

1.  **Sign Up**: Create an account using your email.
2.  **Dashboard**: This is your main hub. It shows your performance overview.

## Core Features

### 1. Adding a Bet
- Click the **"Add Bet"** button (usually in the top right or bottom navigation).
- Fill in the details:
    - **Bet Type**: Win, Place, Each Way, Multi, etc.
    - **Stake**: How much you wagered.
    - **Odds**: The decimal odds (e.g., 2.50).
    - **Date**: When the bet was placed.
    - **Outcome**: Pending, Win, Loss, etc. (You can update this later).

### 2. Managing Bets
- **Edit**: If you made a mistake or need to update the outcome, find the bet in your list and click the edit icon.
- **Delete**: Remove a bet permanently if needed.

### 3. Viewing Stats
- The dashboard updates automatically as you add bets.
- Check your **Profit/Loss**, **ROI** (Return on Investment), and **Strike Rate** (Win %).
- Explore the charts to see your performance over time.

## Beta Testing Tasks

We need your help to ensure everything works perfectly. Please try the following:
- [ ] Add at least 5 different types of bets (Win, Place, Multi).
- [ ] Edit a bet's outcome (e.g., change from Pending to Win).
- [ ] Delete a test bet.
- [ ] Check if the dashboard numbers look correct based on your bets.
- [ ] Try using the app on your phone.

## Reporting Issues

If you find a bug or have a suggestion:
- Take a screenshot if possible.
- Note what you were doing when it happened.
- Send feedback via the "Feedback" button in the app or reply to the beta invite email.
